About PixelFarm

PixelFarm

A simple pixel tracking tool that runs entirely in your browser.

v1.0
Features
Real-time Tracking Live progress bars and countdown timers for every account. Know exactly when each one completes.
Organization Create folders, drag & drop accounts, and customize each card with unique colors and styles.
Full Customization Dark mode, multiple themes, 6 progress bar styles, and 3 languages.
Install as App Add to your home screen for a native app experience. Works offline with full PWA support.
Import & Export Back up your data as JSON files. Import on any device with merge or replace options.
Notifications Get notified when an account reaches full capacity. Never miss a completion again.
Your Privacy Matters
100% local storage — all your data stays on your device using localStorage.
Zero server communication — no data is ever sent to any external server.
No accounts or sign-ups required — open the app and start using it immediately.
No tracking, no analytics, no cookies — your activity is completely private.
Full data control — export, import, or delete all your data at any time.

Built with care. Your data, your control.
